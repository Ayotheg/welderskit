Reporting Suspicious Clients

Akọkọ Ndị Ahịa Na-enyo Enyo

Not every customer is a good one. Protect your business by spotting bad clients early.

Warning Signs (Red Flags)

🚩 Vague Location

If they won't tell you exactly where the job is, be careful. It might be a bait.

🚩 Too Much Hurry

Scammers rush you so you don't notice their tricks. Take your time.

🚩 Strange Payment Requests

If they ask for "registration fees" before they pay you, it's a scam.

How to Report Safely

1. Take Screenshots

Save your chats as proof before you report the client.

2. Use the Report Button

Go to the client's profile on Welder's Kit and click Report.
