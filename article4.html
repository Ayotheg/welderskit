Ịkwụ Ụgwọ Nchekwa

Protect your money. Welder's Kit is a directory, not a payment platform. We never ask for money.

Our Payment Policy

Direct Deals Only

You pay the welder directly. We do not have "payment agents" or escrow services.

No Upfront Full Payment

Never pay 100% of the money before the work starts. This is how people get scammed.

Smart Buying Tips

Buy Materials Yourself

It is safer to buy the iron and rods yourself and bring them to the workshop.

Pay in Milestones

Pay 20% to start, then pay the rest in small parts as the job progresses.

Ètò Isanwo Wa

Isanwo Taara

O n sanwo fun oniweldun taara. A ko ni "awọn aṣoju isanwo".

Ko si Isanwo Kikun Ni Iwaju

Maṣe san ida ọgọrun owo naa ṣaaju ki iṣẹ to bẹrẹ. Eyi ni bi awọn eniyan ṣe n jẹ jibiti.

Ra Ohun-elo Funrarẹ

Ra Irin Funrarẹ

O dara lati ra irin funrarẹ ki o mu lọ si ile-iṣẹ naa.

Sanwo Ni Ipele

San ida ogun fun ibẹrẹ, lẹhinna san iyokù ni ipele bi iṣẹ ṣe n lọ.

Dokar Biyan Mu

Kai Tsaye Zuwa Ga Welder

Kudi kai tsaye ake bawa walda. Ba mu da "wakilan biyan kudi".

Babu Cikakken Biyan Kudi Na Gaba

Kada ka taba biyan kudi 100% kafin a fara aiki. Haka ake damfarar mutane.

Sayi Kayan Da Kanka

Sayi Karfe Da Kanka

Ya fi tsaro ka sayi karfenka da kanka ka kai bitar walda.

Biya Kadan-Kadan

Biya 20% don farawa, sannan ka biya sauran kadan-kadan yayin da aikin ke ci gaba.
