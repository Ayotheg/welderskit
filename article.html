Common Faults

1. Porosity (Tiny Holes)

Small holes from trapped gas. It weakens the structure.

2. Cracks

Most dangerous fault. If you see linear fractures, the weld is bad.

3. Spatter

Molten metal outside the weld area. Makes job look messy.

Quick Tests

Visual Inspection

Look for bubbles, pits, or grooves along the edge.

Tap Test

Tap with a hammer. A clear sound is good. A dull sound means hidden holes.
