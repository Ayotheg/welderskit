Safety Guide for Welders

Itoju Abo fun Oniweldun

Jagoran Tsaro ga Welder

Usoro Nchekwa maka Welder

Protective Gear (PPE)

1. Welding Helmet

Protects eyes from UV rays and sparks. Use auto-darkening lenses.

2. Leather Gloves

Protects hands from intense heat and electric shocks.

3. Flame-Retardant Clothing

Wear long sleeves and pants. No synthetic fabrics (they melt).

Common Hazards

Burns & Sparks

Hot metal burns quickly. Keep a 35-foot clear radius from fires.

Toxic Fumes

Breathing smoke is dangerous. Always work in open, ventilated areas.

Electric Shock

Never weld in wet areas. Keep your equipment dry and insulated.

Safe Workspace

Fire Extinguisher

Always keep a fire extinguisher within 30 feet of your work.

Clean Floor

Remove trash and tools to avoid tripping. No sawdust or oil spills.

Kayan Kare Kai

1. Kwalkwali na Welder

Yana kare idanu daga haske mai kaifi da tartsatsin wuta.

Don kiyaye hannaye daga zafi mai tsanani.

Hatsari Da Ke Akwai

Konewa

2. Glovu Alawọ
