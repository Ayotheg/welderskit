Tips for Hiring Welders

Awọn Imọran fun Biba Oniweldun Lò

Shawarwari don Hayar Walda

Ndụmọdụ maka Iwere Ndị Welder

Finding a good welder doesn't have to be hard. Follow these simple steps to ensure quality work.

1. Check the Badges

✅ Verified Welder

This means we have checked their ID and workshop. Always pick verified welders first.

2. Payment Security

⚠️ Never Pay 100% Upfront

Pay for materials first, then pay for labor as the work finishes. Keep the last payment until you are happy with the job.

3. Inspect the Work

Use the Defector Tool

Before you pay the final balance, scan the welds with our AI tool to check for hidden holes or cracks.

1. Ṣayẹwo Ami Idanimọ

✅ Oniweldun To Daju

Eyi tumọ si pe a ti ṣayẹwo iṣẹ wọn. Nigbagbogbo yan awọn ti o ni ami yii.

2. Ètò Isanwo

⚠️ Maṣe san owo tan lẹẹkan naa

San owo fun ohun elo, lẹhinna san owo iṣẹ bi iṣẹ ṣe n lọ.

1. Duba Tambari

✅ Amintaccen Welder

Wannan yana nufin mun tabbatar da aikinsu. Koyaushe ka zabi wadanda ke da wannan tambarin.

2. Tsarin Biya

⚠️ Kar ka biya kudi gaba daya

Biya kudin kaya tukunna, sannan ka biya kudin kwadago idan aiki ya yi nisa.

1. Lelee Akara (Badges)

✅ Onu Welder Akwadoro

Ebe a pụtara na anyị enyochala ID ha na ebe ọrụ ha. Họrọ ndị nwere akara a mgbe niile.

2. Nchekwa Ịkwụ Ụgwọ

⚠️ Akwụla Ụgwọ 100% n'Ihu

Kwụọ ụgwọ maka ihe eji arụ ọrụ tupu ọrụ amalite, wee kwụọ ụgwọ ọrụ ka ọrụ na-aga n'ihu.

3. Nyochaa Ọrụ Ahụ

Jiri Ngwaọrụ "Defector"

Tupu ị kwụọ ụgwọ ikpeazụ, jiri ngwa AI anyị nyochaa ọrụ ahụ maka ihò zoro ezo ma ọ bụ mgbawa.
