How to Get More Clients

1. Build a Verified Profile

Get the Badge

Upload your ID and workshop photos to get the "Verified" badge. Clients trust verified welders more.

Professional Photo

Use a clear picture of yourself at work. No blurry selfies.

2. Show Your Best Work

Take Quality Photos

Clean your welds before snapping a picture. Use good daylight.

Before & After

Show the broken part and the fixed result. It proves your skill.

3. Good Communication

Reply Fast

Clients usually message 3-4 welders. The first to reply often gets the job.

Be Polite

Speak well to customers. Good manners bring repeat business.
