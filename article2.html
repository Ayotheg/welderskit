Help us keep the community safe! If you see something wrong, report it immediately.

Watch Out for These (Red Flags)

🚩 Fake Photos

Workshops that look like internet pictures instead of real local shops.

🚩 Too Cheap

Prices that are too good to be true usually mean a scam or bad work.

How to Report

1. Click "Report"

Go to the welder's profile and find the report button.

2. Show Proof

Take screenshots of chats or bad work to help us decide.
